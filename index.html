Top Reasons to Say "Yes"

Every site needs a good top 10 list.

# 10

You get a diamond ring!

# 9

There’s a man drought in the world.

# 8

No one will ever mistake your last name for a first name again. Downside is they won't be able to spell or pronounce your new one correctly.

# 7

Forever access to my hoodies. Inexplicably more comfortable than yours.

# 6

If you say yes now, I’ll be a year ahead of the deadline you gave me.

# 5

Our kids would have the bluest eyes.

# 4

Speaking of kids, I will watch Frozen with them over and over again so you won’t have to.

# 3

You can eventually stop watching those trash, reality wedding tv shows (Say Yes to the Dress, Bridezillas, Marriage Boot Camp).

# 2

I’ll pick up your bobby pins for life.

# 1

When things are rough you are the first person I want to be around, and I promise to be there for you.

Other Proposal Ideas Considered

They didn't make the cut.

Where We First Met - I’d pass out from the combination of nerves and Bikram yoga.

At a Major Sporting Event - If you did say no, then it’s up on the Jumbotron for everyone to see.

In a Fancy Restaurant - You know in the movies when the waiter brings the dessert with the ring to the wrong table. That would happen.

Valentine’s Day - It's already been too long of a wait.

Eiffel Tower - Too far away.

In Food - High probability of you eating the ring.

The Rose Petal Trail - Already used it before on something else.

Via Karaoke - I wasn’t that great at SingStar on the easy level.

Flash Dance - I’d look ridiculous smiling at you and trying to dance, while everyone behind me was a professional.

Away on a Holiday - You were already wondering about this one. And too stressful carrying a ring around everywhere.

When you are ready, I have something I want to ask you.
